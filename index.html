About Us

This is what we are.

We are a group of professional in the Robotics filed since 12 years now and have started a Robotics and Automation company 5 years back based on Pune (india). Our major customers are Automobile industry client. We offer electrical maintenance and advanced programming of all types of robot. Experience with different generations of FANUC controllers Handling Tool (RJ3i) to (R30iB).

We have expertise in teaching , programming , quality optimization & on-site training of robots- Fanuc, ABB, KUKA, Motoman, Kawasaki, Nachi, Hyundai etc. Applications worked on- spot welding, arc welding, Hemming, gluing , pick & place, Machine tending etc.
Specialist in Robotic cell relocation activities.

A1 ROBOTIC range of robot engineering services include:
Simulation and Off-Line Programming
On-site Robot Programming
Training
Robot Cell Calibration
Design and Consultancy
Automation Line Shifting, Model Addition, Tact-up Activities
Used Robot Commissioning

We can provide Spares/consumables/services/training for the same servicing entire India. PLC programming expertise with AB (SLC to Controllogix) and Siemens S7-300 series Office.
