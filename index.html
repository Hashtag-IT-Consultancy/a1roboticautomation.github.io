About Us

This is what we are.

We provide maintenance for highly engineered products for Automotive, Aeronautic, Hydraulic and Security applications. With futuristic design, engineering and maintenance facilities, we are able to meet customers exacting requirements. Our facilities are lean, green and clean, and are designed to support neighbouring communities as well as the environment. We offers Industrial Automation services and solutions with Industrial Robots. Owing to our intensive research and customized solutions, we were honoured to associate with high profile clients Apart from Providing the Robotic solutions we provide Integration Services Onsite and Offsite. Our strength lies in speedy implementation at the lowest possible cost.We are established as a reliable automation technology maintenance provider to its customer.
